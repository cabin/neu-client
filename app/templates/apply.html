Is this program for you?

As a program intended to investigate the boundaries of big data across disciplines, we are interested in candidates who have a diverse set of interests.

Prospective students need to be experienced in web and mobile development—curious and imaginative, experimenting with data on their own. They are human-centered practitioners with cross-disciplinary backgrounds and interests. Applicants are equal parts abstract thinker and craftsperson with at least two to five years of professional experience.

Our one-year Master’s in Big Data program takes place on NEU’s campus in Silicon Valley, California. Your application will be approved, and your degree granted, by our esteemed partner, the University of New Haven. Learn more

Application requirements
  1. Bachelor’s degree from a regionally accredited college or university
  2. Official college or university transcripts
  3. Completed online application (applications opening soon)
  4. Two letters of recommendation from instructors or practicing professionals
  5. Statement of purpose (250–500 words) and résumé
  6. Proof of English proficiency (required of applicants whose primary language is not English)
  7. Personal interview (prospective students living beyond a 1,000-mile radius of Palo Alto, California may request a Skype interview)
