Once you complete the application online, all supplemental materials listed below must be submitted to:

Graduate Admissions Office
University of New Haven
300 Boston Post Road
West Haven, CT 06516

  1. The application fee for online applications is currently waived. If you wish to submit a paper application or mail in your completed online application, you must pay a $75 application fee.
  2. Official transcripts forwarded by all colleges previously attended.
  3. Students applying to the Graduate School must submit two letters of recommendation from professional sources. You may now indicate up to three recommenders during the online application process for electronic recommendation submission. If you wish to obtain original letters of recommendation and not use the electronic system, you may do so and mail original copies to the Graduate Admissions Office.
  4. A brief personal statement (approximately 500 words) describing your interest in your intended degree program and why you would make a good candidate to the University of New Haven. Please also include any information that you feel would be important for the admissions committee to be aware of when evaluating your application.
  5. A resume is strongly recommended for all applicants to the Graduate School.
Need help?

If you are having difficulty with the online application, please contact Marcus Hanscom in the Graduate Admissions Office at 203-932-7277 or 1-800-342-5864 x7277.
